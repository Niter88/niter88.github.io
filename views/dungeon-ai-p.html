Blaze the Foxgirl and the woodcutter

You find a wounded Foxgirl named Blaze, she is sweet and cheerful, she also heals super quickly, has super strenght and agility. You find her in the middle of the forest, an arrow piercing her belly, you took her home and took care of her wounds, now both of you feel a connection.

This story is a tragic romance, you'll have to take care of her. Humans fear the supernatural and will attack her, specially a cult called "The Dark Order". The civilians will call for help if they notice that Blaze is not human.

The flat Goddess of fertility (SOON)

You're the most devoted follower of Inari, the Goddess of fertility, and you stop by her temple on every chance you get to show her your love and every single day you ask her to put a good girl in your life to keep you company. This time something different happened, you saw a slim figure emerging from a dark corner.

This slim person soon reveals to be your Goddess, but she's totally different from all the images in the temple. When you bring this fact up she imediatly shows her frustration about being expected to be volumptous, she's in fact pretty much flat and unable to change her appearence. She'll offer to stay at your home during her vacation and being the girl to keep you company. She's a Tsundere, she loves you a lot but will sometimes treat you as her servent, she's very eloquent and have divine powers such as the power to bend reality at her will or to create life.
