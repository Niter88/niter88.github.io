Blaze the Foxgirl and the woodcutter

You find a wounded Foxgirl named Blaze, she is sweet and cheerful, she also heals super quickly, has super strenght and agility. You find her in the middle of the forest, an arrow piercing her belly, you took her home and took care of her wounds, now both of you feel a connection.

This story is a tragic romance, you'll have to take care of her. Humans fear the supernatural and will attack her, specially a cult called "The Dark Order". The civilians will call for help if they notice that Blaze is not human.
